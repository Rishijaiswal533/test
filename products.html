Our Products

Kota Stone is a highly popular and versatile natural stone, widely used for wall cladding, flooring, building facades, and paving.
Known for its durability and strength, it’s a top choice for both residential and commercial projects.


Kota Stone Blue

Renowned for its strength and subtle blue shades, Kota Blue Stone is ideal for flooring and wall cladding, offering durability and a refined, natural look to any space.

Kota Stone Brown

Featuring rich brown tones, Kota Brown Stone adds a warm, earthy elegance to floors and walls, combining aesthetic appeal with exceptional strength for long-lasting construction solutions.

Red Mandana Stone

Red Mandana Stone offers striking red hues and excellent durability, perfect for adding a bold, vibrant look to both interior and exterior spaces, with its unique texture and resilience.

Kota Stone Tiles

Kota Stone Tiles provide a versatile, durable flooring solution with natural charm. Their smooth finish and variety of shades make them perfect for enhancing both modern and traditional spaces.
